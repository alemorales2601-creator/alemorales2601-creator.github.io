Background

A Global Perspective

Originally from Santa Cruz, Bolivia, Hugo Morales moved to the United States to pursue college opportunities through athletics, beginning his journey on an athletic scholarship as a men's soccer student-athlete at Fulton-Montgomery Community College. He now studies Business Economics at the University of Arkansas Walton College of Business, where he combines an international perspective with a strong interest in finance, strategy, and business decision-making.

Alongside school, he founded Elite Level Scouting USA, building a client-based business with measurable revenue, real growth, and a 100% placement rate. His background across entrepreneurship, operations, and cross-cultural environments shapes the way he approaches problems: analytically, competitively, and with long-term ownership in mind.
